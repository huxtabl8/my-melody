Welcome to the World of My Melody!

My Melody is a cute and lovable bunny character created by Sanrio, the company behind other beloved characters such as Hello Kitty and Keroppi. My Melody made her debut in 1975 and has been a beloved character among fans around the world ever since.

My Melody's signature look includes a pink hood with a red ribbon and a pair of round, fluffy bunny ears. She is known for her kind and gentle nature, and loves spending time with her friends in the forest where she lives.
